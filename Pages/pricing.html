Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about my services and process

What format should I send my photos in?

For best results, please send your photos in RAW format if possible. If RAW is not available, high-resolution JPG or TIFF files are acceptable. The higher the original quality, the better the final result will be.

How long does the editing process take?

Turnaround time varies depending on the package you select and current workload. Essential packages typically take 3 days, Professional packages 48 hours, and Elite packages 24 hours. For urgent requests, please contact me directly to discuss expedited options.

What if I'm not satisfied with the edits?

Your satisfaction is my priority. Each package includes revision rounds (Essential: 1 round, Professional: 3 rounds, Elite: unlimited). During revisions, please provide specific feedback on what you'd like modified for best results.

Do you offer industry-specific editing styles?

Absolutely. I specialize in tailoring edits to specific industry standards including Hollywood, Bollywood, Tollywood, Korean drama, and K-pop aesthetics. Please specify your preferred style when submitting your order.

How do I receive my edited photos?

Edited photos are delivered via a secure, password-protected download link. Files are provided in both high-resolution JPG format suitable for printing and web-optimized versions for digital use.
